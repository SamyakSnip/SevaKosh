How SevaKosh Works?

  • Community Contributions: SevaKosh collects funds from residential societies, corporate groups, and individuals who wish to contribute towards meaningful social causes.
  • Execution & Distribution: SevaKosh, in collaboration with local representatives and volunteers, ensures that collected funds are effectively utilized. Each donation drive includes at least one representative from the contributing society, ensuring active involvement and transparency in the distribution process.
  • Transparency & Accountability: Every transaction is documented, and detailed reports, bills, and distribution updates are shared with all contributors. This ensures 100% transparency and donor trust.
  • Growth & Sustainability: To expand its impact, SevaKosh reinvests a small portion of funds into logistics, technology, and outreach efforts, ensuring long-term effectiveness without reliance on external funding.
